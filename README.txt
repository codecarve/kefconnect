KEF Connect for Homey

Control your KEF wireless speakers directly from Homey, bringing premium audio into your smart home automation. Manage volume, sources, and sound settings effortlessly through Homey flows, creating the perfect ambiance throughout your home.

After installing, ensure your KEF speakers are connected to your network, then add them using their IP address. Supports LS50 Wireless II, LSX II, LSX II LT, LS60 Wireless, and XIO Soundbar.
